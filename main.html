Ram-Leela is a 2013 Indian Hindi-language romantic action film directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali. Set in Gujarat, it is an adaptation of Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet, with Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone in the lead roles. The film showcases the passionate love story of Ram and Leela amidst a backdrop of intense family rivalry and violence. Ram-Leela is celebrated for its vibrant visuals, captivating music, and powerful performances, making it a modern-day classic.
